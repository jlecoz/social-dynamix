The Opportunity
The Member Who Walked Out the Door

This story happens at Waterside every time England play. It happens without anyone noticing, without anyone tracking it, and without anyone trying to stop it. Until now.

Tuesday 17 June 2026 · England vs Croatia · 9pm Kick-off
Sarah is a Waterside member. She finishes a 45-minute TechnoGym session, spends 20 minutes in the steam room. It's 7:15pm. She's showered, relaxed, and has 90 minutes before kick-off.

Right now, Sarah gets in her car and drives to the Didsbury Hotel. She stands at a crowded bar for two hours, pays £6.80 a pint, eats nothing. Waterside sees none of that £45 she spends at the pub.

Tonight, we change that. Sarah gets a push notification at 6pm: "England tonight. Match night menu from 7pm. Your table is waiting." She walks upstairs in her gym kit. She orders loaded fries and a glass of wine. She watches the game on the big screen with 70 other members who made the same decision she did.

She spends £38 at Waterside instead of £45 at the pub. She has a better night. You have a better evening's trading.
And on July 19th — the World Cup Final — she's already booked her table three weeks in advance. Because she's not going anywhere else.

Phase 3 · 2027 Onwards
POS & Loyalty Intelligence Layer

Waterside already has a unified POS across at least three zones. Phase 3 takes the transaction data that already exists and turns it into a genuine member intelligence and loyalty system.

"If a member spends £38 on match night, £24 on Sunday lunch, and £15 after spin class — that's £77/month in F&B on top of their membership fee. Right now, Waterside has no way of knowing who these people are or treating them accordingly. Phase 3 makes that data visible and turns it into personalised revenue."
01

Unified Member View

Total spend per member, per zone, per week. Identify the highest-value customers in the building. Treat them like the VIPs they actually are.

02

Loyalty Programme

Spend accumulation across gym, restaurant, bar and spa — rewarded via the existing app. Free guest passes, priority bookings, complimentary spa sessions.

03

Personalised Offers

Sunday swimmer gets a brunch offer. Friday gym-goer gets a bar promotion after spin class. Behaviour-triggered offers delivered via push notification.

04

Membership Conversion

Every World Cup guest is a warm lead. The POS captures their spend. The loyalty programme gives them a reason to sign up. The tournament becomes a 6-week acquisition campaign.
